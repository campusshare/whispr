Our Commitments

Zero-knowledge architecture

We never store IP addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, or any information that can link an account to a real person. Your alias and recovery phrase are yours alone.

Encrypted storage

All evidence files are encrypted at rest using AES-256. Media is served exclusively via signed, expiring Cloudinary URLs with no public access.

Moderation without surveillance

Moderators can see report content but never user identity. All moderation actions are logged in our audit system and reviewed monthly.

Right to be forgotten

Panic Delete permanently and irreversibly removes all data. No backups retain deleted content beyond 24 hours.

No third-party tracking

We use no advertising networks, no analytics pixels, and no social media trackers. The only third-party service is Cloudinary for media storage, with no user tracking enabled.
